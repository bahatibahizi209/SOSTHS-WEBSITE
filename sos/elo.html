ELECTRONIC


Our Electronics program offers students a comprehensive understanding of electronic systems, components, and technologies. This program provides both theoretical knowledge and practical skills in areas such as circuit design, electrical components, and modern electronic devices.

Students will learn how to assemble, test, and troubleshoot various electronic systems, preparing them for real-world applications in fields like consumer electronics, automation, and communication technologies. The program emphasizes hands-on learning, where students gain valuable experience through lab exercises and projects.
