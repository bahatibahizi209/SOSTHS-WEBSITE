About SOS Children’s Villages Rwanda

SOS Children’s Villages Rwanda is an independent member association affiliated to the SOS Federation.
As a leader in childcare, SOS Children’s Villages Rwanda leverages existing community resources to strengthen quality child care and protection through a holistic continuum of care tailored to orphans, vulnerable children and their families. SOS Children’s Villages Rwanda actively advocates for the promotion of childcare and child protection.
